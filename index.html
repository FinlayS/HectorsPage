The show ended after one season which ran for five years, between 1965 and 1970 and produced 78 episodes. This was extrodinarily long for a series commision even by standards in the 60s when the BBC had unlimited funds and an (almost) complete monopoly on the available tv channels. Hector was already 6 when season one began filming. He refused to film a second series citing that he "just wasn't up for it" but rumours were rife that by the time he would have completed a second series, Hector would be well into mid 80s in dog years. Perhaps this was what Hector was referring to when he stated that he wasn't up for it? He refused to expand any further however.
